About Us

A collaboration between Brunei Tourism - True Local is a start up that strives to preserve and provide knowledge on both historical sights and hidden treasures all over Brunei, with a little twist. True Local is built with the community's support and contributions. Discover Brunei like you've never before, explore and become a true local.
